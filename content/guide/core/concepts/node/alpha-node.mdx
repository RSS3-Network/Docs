## Introduction

Alpha Node is an honorary status awarded to RSS3 Nodes that joined the RSS3 Network during the Alpha Mainnet stage from March 12 to June 3, 2024.

The status indicates their early contribution and longstanding support for the Network, and does not offer any special privileges.
