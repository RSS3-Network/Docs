## Introduction

To operate a Normal Node, the Operator is required to pay a deposit into its Operation Pool.

<Callout>
  The current deposit requirement is set to 10,000 $RSS3.
</Callout>

The deposit is a measure to stabilize the Network. In the event of malicious behavior, the Operation Pool including its deposit, is slashed.
