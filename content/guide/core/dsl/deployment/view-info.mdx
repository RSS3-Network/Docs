---
title: View Info
description: Learn how to view your Node's information.
---

To view the status of your Node, visit the [Node Status](https://explorer.rss3.io/nodes/info) page on Explorer.

Enter the endpoint of your Node, such as http://my-node.com/ , and click “GO”. You will then have access to a wealth of valuable information about this Node.
